Multifocal for second lens?

Hi. I was around -6.5 in each eye with 2D of astigmatism in the right eye and 1D of astigmatism in the left eye.

I was diagnosed with a cataract in the right eye around March 2012. In August 2013 I had the cataract in the right eye removed and a monofocal lens was installed – it was a Sensar 8.0 I believe.

I am now +0.75 with 1.0D astigmatism in the right eye.

I believe I have a very slight cataract in the left eye  and am unable to wear contacts for more than a few hours although I am ok wearing glasses even with the large differences in the two eyes.

If I have another monofocal lens in the left eye I now realise I will loose all my intermediate/reading vision, so I am wondering if I should have a multifocal or a trifocal lens installed in the left eye to keep some close up vision.

Or is it better to not mix monofocals with multifocals and just stick with another monofocal lens?
